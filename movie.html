Moana 2

*Moana* is a 2016 Disney animated adventure film that follows the journey of a spirited Polynesian teenager named Moana Waialiki. Chosen by the ocean, she sets sail across the Pacific to save her island from an environmental curse. With the help of the demigod Maui, she seeks to return the heart of Te Fiti, a goddess who brings life to the world. Along the way, Moana discovers her own strength, heritage, and identity as a wayfinder. Blending mythology, breathtaking visuals, and uplifting songs, *Moana* celebrates courage, self-discovery, and the deep connection between people and nature.

Zootopia 2

Zootopia is a 2016 animated film by Disney set in a modern city inhabited by anthropomorphic animals. The story follows Judy Hopps, an ambitious rabbit who becomes the first of her kind to join the Zootopia police force. Determined to prove herself, she teams up with a sly fox con artist, Nick Wilde, to solve a mysterious case of missing predators. As their investigation unfolds, they uncover a larger conspiracy that challenges stereotypes and the harmony of their diverse society. With humor, heart, and a powerful message about inclusion and bias, Zootopia delivers a clever and visually stunning adventure.

Jumanji

Four teens are pulled into a jungle-themed video game, taking on adult avatar roles. To escape, they must survive wild environments, defeat enemies, and work as a team. Along the way, they confront their fears and grow stronger together. Jumanji is an action-filled, humorous film that blends virtual fantasy with real-world lessons of confidence, friendship, and teamwork.

Soul

Joe Gardner is a music teacher who lands his dream jazz gig, but an accident sends him to the "Great Before." There, he meets Soul 22 and rediscovers what makes life meaningful. Soul blends deep themes of purpose and passion with heartfelt moments and music, reminding viewers to cherish ordinary experiences and live fully in the present.

Harold and the Purple Crayon

Harold uses his magical purple crayon to draw worlds of adventure. When lost in his creation, he must rely on his imagination and courage to return home. Along the way, he learns important lessons about creativity and responsibility. The film celebrates the wonder of childhood and the endless possibilities of artistic expression and self-belief.
